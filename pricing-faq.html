FAQs

Explore our frequently asked questions to find answers about our courses and membership plans.

What types of payment do you accept for membership?

We accept major credit and debit cards, including Visa, MasterCard, and American Express, for membership payments.

Is there a trial period for the membership plans?

Yes, we offer a 7-day free trial period for our Monthly and Annual membership plans. The Extended plan does not have a trial period.

Can I cancel my membership at any time?

Absolutely! You can cancel your membership at any time, and there are no cancellation fees. Your access will continue until the end of your billing cycle.

How do I access course materials offline?

Our Monthly and Annual plans include offline viewing. You can download course materials and access them without an internet connection.
